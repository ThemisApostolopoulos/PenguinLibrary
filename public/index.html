Βρες βιβλία με μία αναζήτηση

This site is powered by penguin randomhouse REST API

Instructions:Input in the search form the title or the author's name you wish to search for.Click search.The app will fetch from the penguinrandomhouse REST API the data and via handlebars those results will be rendered in the index.html page.each search containts max 10 results.each result has a save and delete button.If you click the save button the app will save that particular search result in the saved books category, which can be accessed through a link at the top of the search form.If you click the save button while you have already saved the book, the app will alert you.If you press the delete button and that particular search result is saved at your saved books category, the app will delete it.If you press it and the search result isn't saved, you will be alerted.If you go to the saved books category, you can see your saved books, edit their fields or delete them.

Technologies used for this project:html,css,node.js,express,handlebars,nodemon
